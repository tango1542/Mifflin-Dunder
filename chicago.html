Chicago, IL Distribution Center

Mifflin Dunder decided to open up a purely distribution center to meet its ever expanding business. The Windy City of Chicago proved to be the obvious choice to ship goods all over the US. It's central location positions it to be able to properly serve Mifflin Dunder's loyal customer base.

The Chicago location was opened in 1952, and has never looked back, and never looked better. In 2016, extensive damage was caused by a fire that swept through the warehouse, damaging about 30% of all Mifflin Dunder inventory on hand.
